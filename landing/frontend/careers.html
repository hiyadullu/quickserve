The challenges we're up against

Our customers entrust us with the enormous responsibility of helping to run their livelihoods. We work tirelessly to be worthy of that trust. Here's a partial list of things that keep us up at night.

We Are Bandwidth- Constrained

Our business has grown much faster than our company. When we do planning and prioritisation, the projects that end up “below the line” – dropped from near-term plans because of lack of people to staff them – would be exciting, high-impact things to work on. But we maintain an extremely high bar for the quality of what we create and how we interact, and we aren’t prepared to sacrifice that for quantity of output.

Our Growth Depends On Our Users’ Growth

Merely outrunning our competitors would be insufficient – we have to outrun all our customers. We have to get to the regions they want to expand too before they do. We have to support modes of commerce that don’t yet exist because they are still busy inventing them. We have to remain a step ahead of the millions of merchants that use DotPe every day so we help them scale and succeed.

We’re Building For The Long Term

We have to work at the speed of the internet, while also being among the most reliable organisations on it. We need to invent a mode of working which marries the intensity that startups often prize with sustainable careers and organisational memory.
