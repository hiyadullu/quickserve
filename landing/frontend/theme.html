FAQ's

What are the themes?

Themes are fully built web pages containing everything you need to start, like Homepage, catalogue, inner pages and built-in business features. It is fully customisable and can be a great starting point for your website creation journey.

You can find over 1000 designer-made themes from DotPe/Digital Showroom. They cover all types of websites, from a Fashion Store to a Supermarket, an electronics business to a car mechanicand so much more.

Select different themes to play with the design and layout until it fits your wants. You can change the site name, replace images, change colour, add an announcement bar and much more.

You can try any themes for free to see which fits best for your business.

How do I choose a theme?

All the themes are organised into categories for easy browsing.

You can start by looking through the menu to find the category that best describes your business or the website you want to create, like Fashion & Beauty, Electronics, Arts & crafts, Home & Kitchen, Supermarket, or Restaurant.

How to apply a theme?

1. Go to Theme Page

2. Select the category that best fits your business or website idea

3. Click Preview to preview a theme.

4. If you like them, click Apply Theme to use them for your website.

What is the validity of the theme?

The theme once purchased, will be valid for a lifetime.
